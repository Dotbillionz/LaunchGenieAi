05 · Lease negotiation

Nothing verbal. Nothing assumed.

Completed checks are saved on this device.

Negotiation position

  • Request 3–6 months rent-free fit-out.
  • Target deposit of 2–3 months.
  • Tenant break after 12–18 months.
  • Explicit storage, access, CCTV, signage and partition rights.
  • Landlord handles structure and pre-existing non-compliance.
